Queue- FIFO(First-in, First out)
    A queue is a data structure that is based on the FIFO principle when adding and  removing items.
    It have two working workspace:
    1. enqueue(rear) [inserting perfromed from the there]
    2. dequeue(front) [deleting perfromed from the there]

    Basic Operations:
    1. enqueue
    2. dequeue
    3. IsEmpty
    4. IsFull
    5. Peek

    Types of queue:
    1. Simple Queue
    2. Circular Queue
    3. Priority Queue - It's a sort of queue in which each element has a Priority value, 
    4. deque(Dobule )
